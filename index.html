Revolutionizing Production: A Study on AI-Driven Predictive Maintenance in Smart Factories

A Conference Paper by [Your Name]

Abstract

This paper presents a comprehensive study on the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for predictive maintenance within the framework of smart manufacturing. We propose a novel deep learning model that analyzes real-time sensor data from production machinery to predict potential failures with high accuracy. Our findings demonstrate a significant reduction in unplanned downtime and maintenance costs, leading to a more efficient, reliable, and sustainable manufacturing process. The research highlights the transformative potential of AI in modern industrial engineering and offers a practical roadmap for its implementation.
